Notka biograficzna

Joanna Rut graduated master’s and engineering studies as well as doctoral studies at the Faculty of Mechanical Engineering of the Opole University of Technology in Opole, Poland. In 2011, she obtained a doctorate in technical sciences in the discipline of construction and operation of machines. Her main research interests include aspects of management and quality, innovation and implementation processes in industry, IoT, Industry 4.0, Industry 5.0, machine construction and operation, robotic systems, vision systems, smart infrastructure, cybersecurity and diagnostic processes. She is the author and coauthor of over 200 scientific publications, and participated in many scientific and research works completed with implementation in the industry. She gained scientific and practical experience related to the industry by cooperating with enterprises and during the implementation of many scientific internships. In 2011, she received the 3rd degree award of the Rector of the Opole University of Technology for organizational and administrative work. In 2013, she received the 2nd degree award of the Rector of the Opole University of Technology for outstanding scientific achievements. In 2021 she received a pro-quality award of the Rector of the Opole University of Technology for scientific publications for 2019, 2020 and 2021. In 2022 she received a pro-quality award of the Rector of the Opole University of Technology for scientific publications for 2020 and 2021. In 2023 she received a pro-quality award of the Rector of the Opole University of Technology for scientific publications for 2021-2023.
